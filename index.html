Welcome to the North Carolina's Endangered Animals Map

The map below will allow you to explore the endangered animals scattered across North Carolina. All you must do is click on the county you are interested in and it will show you all animals currently listed as endangered. From there simply click on the picture of the species if you are interested in learning more information about it such as what it looks like, the type of habitat it lives in, and why it's endangered.
